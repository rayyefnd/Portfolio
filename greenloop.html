GreenLoop

GreenLoop is a waste management platform that allows users to conveniently schedule trash pickups. As a reward, users earn points that can be redeemed for vouchers or other items. The platform also provides updates on environmental issues, waste education, and sustainability through news and articles displayed on the dashboard.

Challanges

During the development process, we often faced challenges such as difference of ideas, technical issues, and business logic. At first, it was difficult to keep tasks on schedule, and we even had to redo the entire plan. To overcame this, we organized daily brainstorming sessions after class, reviewed successful past projects, and analyzed feedback from our lecturer during weekly presentations. We also worked on keeping documentation clear, which helped improve communication and allowed the team to collaborate more smoothly.
