Traffic Management System

This system is designed to detect the number of vehicles at an intersection in order to reduce congestion on one side of the lane. The system determines which lanes have heavier congestion and adjusts the green light accordingly. This system is also able to detect special vehicles such as ambulances, fire trucks, and government vehicles, allowing them to pass through the intersection without delay by giving them priority
